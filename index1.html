Letter Linker: The Game


Welcome to Letter Linker!


Game instructions:

  • At the beginning of the round, the player will need to fill in 8 letters in the given boxes. In order to do this, the player would choose if they would like a vowel (A, E, I, O, U) or consonant (any other letter), which is randomly drawn from the suitable set of letters.

  • After the letters have been picked, the player has 45 seconds to figure out as many valid words as they can from the letters drawn.

  • At the end of the round, the words are shown and if they are valid, the player is awarded 1 point per letter in a valid word. After time has ended, the vaild words and their corresponding points are displayed.

  • If the player wants to play again, a restart button will be shown.
